What happens next

A human follows up while the pilot is still manual

  1. Tell us whether you are a driver needing help or a provider with coverage.
  2. Share the rough location, timing, and charging details needed for follow-up.
  3. Oasis Now reviews the request manually and looks for reachable local options.
  4. We use each real response to decide what the full mobile app should automate first.

Why this can be worth more

Urgency changes the price of the same service

  • A charger tomorrow is cheap. A reachable solution before you are stranded is valuable.
  • Distance, ETA, trust, and availability matter as much as the posted charging price.
  • Providers who can respond quickly should be discoverable when the need is time-sensitive.
  • The first Oasis Now category is EV charging, but the core marketplace is urgent local fulfillment.
